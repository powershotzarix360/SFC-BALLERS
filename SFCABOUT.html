About SFC Ballers

Founded in 2016 in the heart of Shah Faisal Colony, Karachi, SFC Ballers began as a dream shared by local football enthusiasts. What started as a few matches between neighbors has grown into a powerful club competing in regional tournaments and inspiring youth.

Vision: To become Karachi’s most dynamic youth football club, offering opportunity, discipline, and growth to young athletes from all backgrounds.

Mission: We aim to foster teamwork, passion, and excellence by providing structured training, regular competition, and community support.

Message from the Founder:
"SFC Ballers is not just a club — it’s a family. We are driven by love for the game and a desire to uplift our community through football. Together, we play hard, train smart, and win with honor."
